What is Fini?
Fini is an awesome new game that let’s you finish your friends’ tweets by guessing the missing word.

Getting started is a breeze. After downloading the app and signing in with your Twitter user name and password, your timeline will load and you’re ready to start guessing.
Compete against your friends.
If you can guess the right word to finish a tweet, you gain points. Accumulating points will award you with impressive achievements.
